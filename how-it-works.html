FAQ

Is this an official municipal app?
No. This is a private community network. Neighbours helping neighbours is the fastest way to solve "Bin Day" stress.
Why is the map free?
Residents are the ones providing the data by logging the truck. It makes sense that tracking is free for everyone. The Pro upgrade covers server costs and keeps the app ad-free for non-Pro users.
What do I get with Pro?
A push notification sent directly to your phone the moment the truck is reported near your street — even when the app is closed. No more checking the app manually.
