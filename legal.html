Terms & Privacy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Privacy Policy (Your Data)

At Coetzee Data AI Consultants (Pty) Ltd, we value your privacy. Here is how we handle your information:

  • Location Data: We only access your GPS when you actively log a truck sighting, to record the approximate location of the report. We do not track you in the background.
  • Anonymity: Your reports are anonymous to other users. They see only a green dot on the map — not your name, device, or house number.
  • Local Storage: Your membership status (Free or Pro) and ping count are stored locally on your device only and are not linked to a personal account.
  • No Data Selling: We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Push Notifications: If you enable alerts (Pro feature), your browser handles the notification. No personal contact details are stored by us.

3. Free Access & Pro Subscription
